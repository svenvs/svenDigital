Sven van Straalen

Profile

Sven is an Architect who can’t be separated from code. He is always looking for awesome new solutions. Sven is a specialist on development and new technology. He is always eager to learn about new frameworks and technologies and puts a lot of energy into this. He attends conferences and reads magazines on web development in his free time. Because of his expertise, colleagues often come to Sven to ask for advice or when they have questions about their work. Sven really enjoys to help others with his knowledge on a daily basis.​ Together with his Agents of C.O.D.E he manages a great development community within Capgemini, where tech events like hackathons, meetups and knowledge sessions are organized. Sven is also outside Capgemini being recognized for his technical knowledge where he is invited on several speaking engagements on tech conferences like Jfall, Angular NL, Front end Love, .BLD() and tech fair
